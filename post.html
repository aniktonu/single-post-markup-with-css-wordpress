Project Details

Project Name : Supply, Installation, Testing, & Commissioning of Requisition & Distribution System for Water Treatment Plant for Payra Port
Client : Payra Port
Project Scope: The Payra Port authority thought to have such a solution that might help them to distribute water to their all customers in optimal and automated way. To fulfil their goal, Intellier Limited has supplied & installed a distribution system for Payra Port authority. The functionalities of the water distribution system is to deliver water to all customers of the system in sufficient quantity for potable drinking water and fire protection purposes, at the appropriate pressure, with minimal loss, of safe and acceptable quality, and as economically as possible.
Project/Service Type : Web Application
Technology Used: Angular, Typescript
